2007

Humble Beginnings: Ryan and Isaac are born; they will soon embark on a journey as rich as gold and as old as time. Only a genie could predict what would happen next in their lives.

2015

First Bakery Opened: Isaac convinced Ryan to move to Norway and open "Starbucks." This business was founded on the principles of honor, loyalty, and being courageous. These are simply traits valued by the owners.

2016

Isaac and Ryan lose Starbucks due to a lawsuit that exposed the horrid working conditions of most of the locations on the North American continent

2018

Ryan tells Isaac to get his money up, not his funny up. Ryan telling Isaac this changed Isaac's perspective on life and the universe in the general. Isaac decides to move to Coasta Rica and Ryan shortly follows.

2020

Ryan finds an ancient tomb in Costa Rica and finds a supply of ancient coffee beans. Once Ryan had brewed a simple espresso using these beans, he knew immedietly that he should open a coffee house and use these beans.

2021

Life in Costa Rica was not always so lavish Ryan and Isaac worked hard in order for Prehistoric Pours to succesed as a business. From their hard work they we able to claim the fruits of their own labor, they were their own boss.

Now

Currently both memebers continue to live on Isla Nublar and work the coffeehouse. They each have their own desires in life and may continue to run Prehistoric Pours, or perhaps a new journey will begin in the future.
